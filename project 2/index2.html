Springfields Secondary School

Springfields School of Education is a school community with a mission. Our great desire is to provide international education to all the students of Zambia at an affordable fee without compromising the quality of our education. Education is still a luxury than a right in most parts of the world and especially good education offering international qualification is a privilege very few would enjoy.We want to contribute to the community and the world at large by providing our students opportunities to learn and seek wisdom.
